🔐 RSA Key Exchange Overview

A high-level visualization of how RSA encrypts and decrypts data securely.

This diagram demonstrates the flow of information in RSA: the sender encrypts a message with the recipient's public key, and the recipient decrypts it with their private key. This ensures confidentiality and prevents unauthorized access to the message.
